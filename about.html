I Found Me

My name is Michael Gray and I was born and raised in Houston Texas. I graduated from Westfield High School in 2004, and soon after joined the United States Army. While in the army I went to the war in Iraq twice, once in 2006, and again in 2008. After my second tour to Iraq I left the military and began to persue my career in computer science at the University of Mary Hardin-Baylor. I've always known that I loved math and computers, but my time in college showed me how to put them together in a powerful way.
